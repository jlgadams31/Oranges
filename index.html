Legacy, Stewardship, Quality


Franklin Groves was founded in 1953 with 35 acres in the San Joaquin Valley of California. Today, Franklin Groves is a 4th generation family owned and operated world class citrus producer, farming acreage from Orange Cove to Maricopa.

California agriculture, and especially citrus, is a fast paced modern endeavor. Franklin Groves applies the latest in advanced technology and a strong work ethic to meet the needs of our customers, health of our groves, and challenges of farming.

Our passion is to provide our consumers with a consistent, ultra-premium orange and our mission is to keep our groves healthy for future generations.
